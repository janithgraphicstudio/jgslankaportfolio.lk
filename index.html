Frequently Asked Questions

How many days does it take to create a logo?

On average, it takes 2-3 days to develop the initial concepts. The entire process, including revisions and final delivery, takes 5-7 days. The duration may vary based on your requirements and the number of revisions.

What file types will I receive for my design?

Once approved, you will receive the final files in the format you need (High-Quality) files.

  • AI (Adobe Illustrator Vector file)
  • EPS (Vector file)
  • PNG (Transparent Background)
  • JPG (High-Resolution)
  • SVG (Web Vector)

For Social Media Post, you will receive a High-Quality PNG or JPG file.

What payment process do you follow? (Payment Process)

Once the project starts, you will receive an advance payment of 50% of the total amount. The remaining 50% will be paid after the final files are delivered. You can pay through Bank Transfer.

What revisions can I expect during the design process?

My main priority is to provide 100% satisfaction. If you have any additional requirements, we can discuss and create new concepts to meet your needs. Throughout the project, we may need to make revision rounds to ensure you receive the desired results.
